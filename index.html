About Goshen

Founded by Jonathan Mudenyo from Webuye, Goshen nurtures faith, education, and sport for unprivileged talented children.

Meaning of Goshen

Goshen was a safe place of plenty in ancient Egypt given to Jacob and his descendants. We aim to recreate that safe place for children today.

Purpose

We help children fill the void of rejection or lost dreams with God and their talents, steering them away from drugs and crime.
